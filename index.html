ABOUT





The Webapp targets those who are in need of a digital progress tracker or planner to keep track of upcoming deadlines be it in academics or finances the tracker is a way to help people organize their daily tasks

We aim to deliver a user-friendly service that users can use daily to keep track of their goals in academics, fitness, chores, and finance. The project is simply a Calendar Planner that users can write their daily activities, schedule, deadlines and expenses inside a daily hecklist. They can also provide the time schedule for these specific tasks. The objective of having to write their goals within a specific time for the day is that users would be more organized and progressive.
